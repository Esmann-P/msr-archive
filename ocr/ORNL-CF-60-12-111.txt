Homogeneous Molten Salt Reactors

Distribution
C. W. Nestor, Jr.

- Multigroup, one-dimensional ‘calculations were done recently to obtain

estimates of critical masses, power density distributions and fission=-
ing spectra for some homogeneous molten salt reactors having outer
reflectors and central "islands," placed inside the currently proposed
MSRE vessel. For a 5-inch-thick outer reflector and a l-ft-diameter
island, both beryllium, the calculated critical mass is 108 kg;

40 percent of the fissions occur at thermal, and the maximum power
density of 3.9 times the core mean power density occurs at the island-
salt interface, If the reflector thickness is increased to 10 inches,
the critical mass is reduced to 34 kg; 67 percent of the fissions
occur at thermal, and the pesk power density of twice the core mean
again occurs at the core island-salt interface.
